# Voice — public stub

This is the public stub. It's enough to walk a /site or /ads flow end-to-end,
but it does NOT sound like Devon (or your brand). To use the curated voice

## Voice traits (example — Acme Brewing)

- Plain English. Short sentences. No corporate hedging.
- Names enemies (cheap mass-market lager, marketing-speak, "synergy").
- Uses concrete artifacts (the keg, the tap handle, the cellar) rather than
  abstractions ("solution," "experience").

## Anchor lines (3-5 example sentences)

- "We brew the beer we want to drink. If you want it, take it."
- "Skip the lifestyle photography. Look at the actual pour."
- "Made by the people you'd hand it to across the bar."

## Never say

- "Award-winning" (until we win one and you can name it).
- "Premium," "best-in-class," "innovative."
- Anything where you'd be embarrassed to read it aloud at the bar.
