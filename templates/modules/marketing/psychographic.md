## How to Build This

1. **Interview 5-10 customers** — Ask about their journey, not just the product
2. **Look for patterns** — What beliefs do they share?
3. **Define the negative** — Who should NOT buy?
4. **Write the one-sentence** — Force clarity
5. **Update quarterly** — As your business evolves, so does your customer
