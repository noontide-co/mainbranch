## How to Build This

1. **Collect 10-20 testimonials** — DMs, reviews, community posts
2. **Extract verbatim phrases** — Copy exact words, don't paraphrase
3. **Categorize by intent** — What are they trying to say?
4. **Note patterns** — What words repeat across customers?
5. **Update monthly** — Add new phrases as you collect them

**Rule:** If you're writing copy and it doesn't sound like something in this bank, you're probably off-voice.
