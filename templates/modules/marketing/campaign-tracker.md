## Metrics Glossary

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| CPA | $ | Cost per acquisition |
| CTR | >1% | Click-through rate |
| CPM | <$ | Cost per 1000 impressions |
| Conversion Rate | % | Signups / clicks |
| ROAS | X | Return on ad spend |

---

## Batch Naming Convention

`{batch#}_{format}_{angle}_{date}`

**Format codes:**
- `IMG` — Static image ads
- `VID` — Video ads
- `CAR` — Carousel ads
- `VSL` — Video sales letter

**Examples:**
- `001_IMG_overwhelm-clarity_2026-01-15`
- `002_VID_framework-focus_2026-01-20`
- `003_VSL_transformation_2026-02-01`

---

## Review Cadence

- **Daily:** Check spend and obvious winners/losers
- **Weekly:** Full batch review, update this tracker
- **Monthly:** Angle-level analysis, update angles/README.md

---

## How to Use This

1. **Before launch:** Add campaign to Active Campaigns
2. **After 3-5 days:** Move to Winners or Losers with notes
3. **Extract learnings:** What did you learn? Add to Learnings section
4. **Iterate:** Use winners to inform next batch
