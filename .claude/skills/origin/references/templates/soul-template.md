## Distillation Guidelines

**Pull from origin.md:**

| Origin Section | Soul Section |
|----------------|--------------|
| The Spark | Why I Do This |
| The Wisdom | What I Bring |
| The Values | Values (Non-Negotiable) |
| The Trials (trench stories) | Trench Stories |
| The Vision | The Vision |

**The Test questions:**
These may not be in origin.md. If not, ask the user:
- "What do you research when no one's watching?"
- "What intersections excite you when you find them?"
- "What decisions feel like discovery vs obligation?"

**Tone:**
- Even more condensed than origin.md
- Punchy, direct
- Easy to scan in 5 minutes
- Should feel like a manifesto

**Length:**
- 300-500 words total
- Scannable sections
- Placeholder for future trench stories
