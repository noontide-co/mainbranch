# Script Templates & Hook Bank

## Script Templates

### Template 1: Problem-Solution

```
HOOK:
Most [audience] are doing everything right and still feel completely stuck.

BODY:
It's not effort that's missing. It's a framework.

[Context about scattered approaches]

[Your solution] changes that.

[Key mechanism or unique approach]

When you know [key insight], you stop guessing.

CTA:
[Offer name] is [price]. Cancel anytime.

Link in bio to join.
```

### Template 2: Testimonial-Led

```
HOOK:
"[Quote from testimonial]"

That's [Name]. [Brief context].

BODY:
[Their situation before]

Then [they found your solution].

Here's what [they] said:

"[Specific result quote]"

That's what happens when you have [mechanism] instead of [old way].

CTA:

[Price]. [Benefit included].
```

```
HOOK:
[Doing X] can feel incredibly isolating.

BODY:
[Empathy for their situation]

[Pain point 1]. [Pain point 2]. [Pain point 3].

And [the mental load]? It's constant.

Inside [offer], there are [community benefit].

You don't have to figure this out alone.
```
