## How Much Detail Do You Need?

**Minimum (to get started):**
- Tone (2-3 words)
- 3 "never say" phrases
- Energy level

**Better (for consistent output):**
- Everything above plus vocabulary
- Example phrases from your actual content

**Best (for scripts that truly sound like you):**
- All sections filled
- 5+ example phrases
- Quirks documented

Start minimal. Add more as you use `/organic` and notice what's missing.
