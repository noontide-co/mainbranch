## Step 3: Market Drivers (Buying Motives)

**[3:00 - 4:30]**

Let me walk you through why most facility managers come to us.

**The number one reason: They're tired of the cycle.**

They've hired cleaning companies before. Starts great. Quality drops after a few months. They complain. Nothing changes. Six months later, they're back on Google looking for someone new.

The time cost alone is significant. Interviews. Proposals. Onboarding. Training the new company on your facility. If your time is worth anything, that cycle is expensive — even if the cleaning itself is cheap.

**The second reason: Accountability.**

Most facility managers have no real visibility into whether their cleaning actually happened. They show up in the morning. It looks okay. But did every task get done? They don't know. They're hoping.

Our tracking system eliminates that. You can see exactly what got cleaned and when. No guessing.

**The third reason: Protection.**

Especially for medical facilities. Slip and fall. Water damage. Security issue. If your cleaning company only carries a million in coverage and something goes wrong, you're exposed.

We carry ten million. That's not marketing — it's policy. And for facility managers in healthcare or legal, that matters.

**The fourth reason: The floors.**

This one's specific, but I hear it constantly. Everything else looks fine, but the floors never look right. Scuff marks. Buildup. That film that won't go away.

It's usually a frequency and tracking problem. We schedule floor care properly and verify it happens. It's not complicated — but most companies don't do it.

**[5:45 - 7:00]**

Let me address the questions I get most often.

That's the whole point of the tracking system. Quality drops when there's no accountability. When tasks are tracked and verified every night, problems get caught immediately — not three months later when you're frustrated enough to fire us.

We've had clients for over ten years. They stay because the system works.

**"What if we have an issue?"**

You'll have my direct number. If something's wrong, we fix it the next day. That's the commitment.

Most cleaning companies are hard to reach. We're not. Responsiveness is built into how we operate.

**"Why is your insurance so high? Is that a red flag?"**

It's the opposite. High insurance means we take liability seriously. It means if something happens, you're actually protected — not left holding the bag with a company that can't cover the damage.

We've never had a claim. But if we did, you'd be glad we're covered.

**"Can you handle our specific requirements?"**

If you're a medical practice, law firm, or commercial office in Austin — yes. These are our specialties. We understand the compliance requirements, the patient and client sensitivity, and the details that matter in professional environments.

If you're a warehouse, manufacturing plant, or residential property — we're probably not the right fit. We focus on what we're best at.
