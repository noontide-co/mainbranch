## Structure

| Slide | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Hook (stops scroll) |
| 2 | Context/Setup |
| 3-7 | Value (1 idea per slide) |
| 8-9 | Summary |
| 10 | CTA |

---

## Copy Limits

- **Headline:** 5-10 words max
- **Body:** 20-30 words/slide
- **Rule:** If >2 seconds to read, too long

---

## Swipe Motivation

- End slides with incomplete thoughts
- Use "..." or arrows
- Number slides to show progression

---

## Carousel Types

| Type | Slides | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| List/Tips | 7-10 | How-to, numbered |
| Story | 6-8 | Journey, transformation |
| Myth-Busting | 8-10 | Debunking |
| Comparison | 6-8 | This vs That |
| Mini-Guide | 10+ | Deep dives |

---

## Caption Guidelines

- Expand (don't repeat) slide content
- Add context/personal story
- Ask question for comments
- End with CTA
- Length: 100-300 words
