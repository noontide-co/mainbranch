## Voice Checklist

- [ ] Sounds like creator (not copywriter)
- [ ] Uses their vocabulary
- [ ] Matches energy level
- [ ] Contractions natural
- [ ] No AI tells
- [ ] Avoids "never say" list

---

## Adaptation Rules

1. **Keep structure** - Framework that worked
2. **Change specifics** - Examples, numbers, details
3. **Add voice** - Vocabulary, energy, quirks
4. **Connect to offer** - How it relates

Never copy hooks verbatim. Adapt PATTERN, not words.
