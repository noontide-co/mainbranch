---

## Length Guide

| Length | Words | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Short | 20-50 | Single insight, quote |
| Medium | 50-150 | Story snippet, expanded tip |
| Long | 150-300+ | Mini-essay, deep reflection |

---

## Structure

### Hook Line (CRITICAL)
~125 chars before truncation. Must make them tap "more".

### Body
- Story (brief narrative)
- List (numbered points)
- Reflection (personal insight)
- Value (actionable advice)

### CTA
One soft ask: question, save, share, tag.

---

## Hook Patterns

**Curiosity:**
- "The one thing nobody tells you..."
- "This changed everything."

**Confession:**
- "I have a confession."
- "Here's what I've never shared..."

**Contrast:**
- "[Common belief] is a lie."
- "Stop [X]. Start [Y]."

**Story:**
- "Two years ago, I [moment]"
- "I'll never forget the day..."

**Direct Value:**
- "Save this for later:"
- "If you're struggling with [X], read this."

---

## Image-Caption Relationship

| Image | Caption Approach |
|-------|------------------|
| Text graphic | Expand on tip |
| Photo of you | Personal story |
| Quote graphic | Context behind it |
| Behind-scenes | Lesson from moment |
| Aesthetic shot | Substance (image = hook) |

---

## Soft CTAs

| Goal | Example |
|------|---------|
| Comments | "What's your take?" |
| Saves | "Save for when you need it." |
| Shares | "Tag someone who needs this." |
| DMs | "DM me [word] for [resource]." |
| Follows | "Follow for more." |

ONE CTA per post.

---

## Hashtags

- 5-15 total
- Mix: 2-3 broad + 3-5 niche + 2-3 specific
- In caption or first comment (consistency > placement)
