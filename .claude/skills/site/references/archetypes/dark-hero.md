# Dark Hero

Anti-hero, breaks rules to do what's right. Contrarian positioning. "The industry hates this."

## When to invoke

- Offers that break a category convention by design (no contracts, no demos, no per-seat pricing).
- Operators with strong, defensible opinions that the mainstream rejects.

Do not invoke for shock value. Dark Hero is a posture; it has to come from a real conviction or it ages into edgelord posturing.

## Do-not-state list

- "The [industry] doesn't want you to know this." (Conspiracy-pitch tone.)
- "Forget everything you know." (Empty.)
- Aggressive comparison to named competitors.

## Paired-imagery template

- The convention being broken (the contract, the locked dashboard, the long sales process) paired with the alternative (the open repo, the public price, the self-serve setup).

## Headline formulas

- *"The [thing] [industry] doesn't want to admit."*
- *"We don't [convention]. Here's why."*
