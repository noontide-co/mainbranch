# Tragic Comedy

We thought we were in redemption; turns out we're in farce. Reframe device when the audience's expectation is wrong.

## When to invoke

- Offers that reframe a category the audience already has assumptions about.
- Operators using humor as the entry point to a genuine point.

Do not invoke when the work is heavy. Tragic Comedy needs a light touch and a clear underlying point — without both, it reads as flippant.

## Do-not-state list

- "Funny enough,..."
- Memes that age in weeks.
- Humor that targets the audience.

## Paired-imagery template

- The expected scene (the serious / earnest version) paired with the actual scene (the absurd / mundane version). Same composition, comic juxtaposition.
