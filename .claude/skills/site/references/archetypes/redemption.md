# Redemption

Bad guy goes to jail, wrong gets righted. Transformation offers — but **don't promise the ending**.

## When to invoke

- As a narrative spine for the operator's story, not as a promise to the buyer.
- When the audience needs to see that this kind of change is possible.

## Do-not-state list

- "We'll make you [end state]." (Promising the ending invites failure.)
- "Guaranteed transformation."
- "Before / after" headlines without specifics.

## Paired-imagery template

- Mid-arc imagery (the work in progress) rather than before/after pairs. Skip the cliche.

## Reframe target

- Pair with `archetype: wounded-healer` (the operator already walked it) or `archetype: broken-hero` (the audience is mid-arc and trying again).
